🌬️ Breathing Exercises

Follow this guided breathing method to calm your mind:

  • Inhale deeply for 4 seconds
  • Hold your breath for 7 seconds
  • Exhale slowly for 8 seconds
  • Repeat for 5 cycles

Breathe in... Hold... Breathe out...

🎵 Sound Meditation

Use soothing sounds or mantras to focus and relax:

  • Play nature sounds, calming music, or chants
  • Focus your attention on the sound alone
  • If distracted, gently return to the sound

🎨 Visualization

Close your eyes and imagine peaceful scenes like:

  • A quiet beach with waves gently rolling
  • A forest with birds chirping
  • A warm glowing light enveloping you

✨ Guided Meditation

Use voice-guided sessions to walk through calming scenarios:

  • Helps beginners stay focused
  • Available through apps, videos, or audio clips
